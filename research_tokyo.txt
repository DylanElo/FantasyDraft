1. YUTA OKKOTSU
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Yuta Okkotsu is a Special Grade sorcerer and one of the four Special Grade
sorcerers recognized by Jujutsu High. By the end of the series he is arguably
the most versatile sorcerer alive, functioning as a copy-and-amplify engine
with near-limitless cursed energy supplied through Rika.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
CORE ABILITY: RIKA (Cursed Spirit / Queen of Curses)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Origin: Rika Orimoto was Yuta's childhood friend who died in an accident and
became bound to him as a cursed spirit. In JJK 0 (prequel) she exists as a
massive, monstrous incomplete cursed spirit of unquantifiable cursed energy.
At the end of JJK 0, Yuta releases her soul so she can rest in peace.

In the main series (post-Culling Game), Yuta reconstructs Rika from his own
boundless cursed energy. This new Rika is not a lingering spirit -- she is a
cursed spirit that Yuta actively generates and maintains from his own reserves.
She is his primary power source and the vessel through which he performs Copy.
She can manifest physically as a massive entity to fight, protect, or amplify.

Damage type: Physical (manifestation attacks), Energy (cursed energy projection)
Debuff: None inherently; her presence suppresses opponents psychologically
Unique property: Because Yuta generates Rika himself rather than inheriting
her, she has effectively no cursed energy ceiling -- she is as powerful as
Yuta's reserves allow. His reserves are described as among the largest of any
living sorcerer.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
CORE ABILITY: COPY (Innate Cursed Technique)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Yuta's innate cursed technique. He can copy and store any cursed
technique he witnesses or experiences directly, using Rika as the medium to
store and replicate the copied technique. He does not need to fully understand
the technique -- Rika retains it and allows him to reproduce it.

Limitations: Cannot copy techniques requiring a bloodline or physiology he
does not possess. He copies technique-based outputs, not inheritable genetics.
Damage type: Varies by copied technique
Unique property: He can use multiple copied techniques in a single engagement.
By the Shinjuku arc, he has a large stored library of techniques.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONFIRMED COPIED TECHNIQUES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

A) CURSED SPEECH (copied from Toge Inumaki)
   Mechanics: Inumaki's lineage technique -- words spoken with cursed energy
   compel reality to obey their literal meaning. Yuta copies this and uses it
   with a megaphone in JJK 0 to amplify the command volume and range.
   Confirmed command used: "Twist" (Nejire) -- causes the target's body to
   internally collapse and contort catastrophically.
   Damage type: Affliction (soul-level compulsion, bypasses physical defenses)
   Debuff: "Don't Move"/"Stop" = full paralysis/stun. "Twist" = lethal internal
   structural damage. Commands scale in power with cursed energy output.
   Unique property: Yuta appears to use Cursed Speech without the same severe
   throat damage Inumaki suffers, likely because Rika mediates the output cost.

B) CLEAVE (copied from Ryomen Sukuna)
   Mechanics: An invisible adaptive slash of cursed energy. The cut
   automatically scales its power to match the target's durability and cursed
   energy level. Against an uncursed human, one slash is instantly lethal.
   Against a hardened Grade 1 sorcerer, it scales proportionally stronger.
   Damage type: Affliction-class (cursed technique slash, not a physical blade;
   bypasses conventional physical defenses)
   Debuff: None -- pure adaptive damage
   Unique property: Cleave's power is not fixed -- it adjusts automatically.
   This makes it equally effective against weak and powerful targets alike.

C) DISMANTLE (copied from Ryomen Sukuna)
   Mechanics: Fixed-output cursed energy slash (unlike the adaptive Cleave).
   Produces multiple small cutting slashes simultaneously that fan outward.
   Used for area suppression or multi-hit attacks against single targets.
   Damage type: Affliction-class (cursed technique cutting damage)
   Debuff: None -- pure fixed damage
   Unique property: More controllable than Cleave. Yuta uses Dismantle in the
   Shinjuku arc. The fixed output means it is less situationally variable.

D) DHRUV LAKDAWALLA'S TECHNIQUE -- VECTOR
   Mechanics: Dhruv's CT manipulates vectors (direction and magnitude of
   forces). Any force (physical strikes, cursed energy blasts, projectiles)
   that contacts the user can be redirected, amplified, or fully negated.
   Damage type: Physical (redirected kinetic force) or Energy (redirected
   cursed energy blasts); redirected attacks carry the original damage value
   Debuff: None directly, but attackers can be hit by their own redirected
   attacks -- a de facto counter
   Unique property: Extremely powerful defensively. Yuta using Vector means
   any physical technique directed at him can theoretically be turned back on
   the attacker. One of the most useful defensive copies in his arsenal.

E) REVERSE CURSED TECHNIQUE (RCT) / HEALING
   Mechanics: Yuta can use RCT to heal himself and others. Positive cursed
   energy (the inversion of negative cursed energy) is channeled to regenerate
   wounds, restore flesh, and cure cursed damage.
   Damage type: N/A -- healing
   Debuff: N/A
   Unique property: Very few sorcerers can use RCT to heal others. Shoko Ieiri
   is the primary known exception outside Yuta. He can apply RCT through Rika
   to heal allies mid-combat. His enormous reserves mean sustained healing is
   viable in extended fights.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
KATANA / SWORD COMBAT
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Yuta is an elite swordfighter. He wields a katana and is noted as
physically exceptional in close combat even when not using techniques.
His swordsmanship is enhanced by cursed energy output channeled through Rika,
making each slash carry enormous force beyond a normal blade strike.
Damage type: Physical (blade cutting)
Debuff: None
Unique property: His sword is frequently infused with his copied techniques
(e.g., striking with a blade while applying Cleave or Dismantle simultaneously
for compounded damage output).

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
TRUE FORM: RIKA
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: When Yuta releases Rika fully -- pouring his cursed energy into her
without restriction -- she manifests as a massive ancient-power-level entity.
In this state her raw cursed energy output is at Special Grade level or above.
Damage type: Physical + Energy (massive combined output)
Debuff: The sheer cursed energy pressure can stun/suppress weaker opponents
Unique property: Yuta's own body is protected inside/alongside Rika during
this state. She functions simultaneously as extreme offense and defense.
This is Yuta's ultimate form; the cost is enormous cursed energy expenditure.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DOMAIN EXPANSION: AUTHENTIC MUTUAL LOVE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Yuta's domain creates a bounded space filled with Rika's
overwhelming cursed energy. Unlike most damage-dealing domains (e.g., Malevolent
Shrine's slashes, Unlimited Void's information flood), Authentic Mutual Love
is an AMPLIFICATION domain. Its sure-hit property ensures every technique Yuta
uses inside automatically makes contact -- Rika guarantees this.
Effect on targets: All Yuta's copied techniques (Cleave, Dismantle, Cursed
Speech commands) cannot be dodged or blocked through movement. They land with
mathematical certainty due to the domain's guaranteed-hit property.
Damage type: Varies -- the domain itself does not deal a specific damage type;
it is an amplification chamber for Yuta's diverse technique library.
Debuff: All techniques used inside gain sure-hit property
Unique property: Has an open/incomplete deployment (used as a barrier or aura)
and a full territorial expansion. The full expansion places all targets inside.
Unlike most domains, the "hit" is not a single effect but a guarantee that
all his diverse copied techniques land simultaneously or in sequence.

2. SATORU GOJO
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Satoru Gojo is the strongest jujutsu sorcerer in the world at the time of the
series. He possesses both the Limitless cursed technique and the Six Eyes --
a combination that appears once per generation in the Gojo clan. His power
is described as a systemic threat to the entire jujutsu establishment.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
CURSED TECHNIQUE: LIMITLESS (Mugen)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Gojo's innate technique manipulates space at an atomic/mathematical level.
It operates by applying the concept of infinite convergence to real space.
Three distinct states:

A) INFINITY (Passive / Active Defense)
   Mechanics: The baseline state of Limitless. Gojo slows all matter
   approaching him to infinitely small increments using the mathematical
   concept of convergence (Zeno's paradox applied physically). Nothing ever
   fully reaches him -- the gap between an attack and Gojo always contains an
   infinite number of subdivisions, so impact is mathematically impossible.
   Damage type: None -- purely defensive
   Debuff: None applied to enemies
   Active vs. Passive: Normally runs passively (always on). Gojo can
   consciously deactivate it (he does this to conserve energy or allow allies
   to touch him). He can also tune Infinity selectively -- allowing some things
   through while stopping others.
   Unique property: This is not a "shield" with HP -- it is a mathematical
   law applied to space. It cannot be broken through by raw force alone.
   The Six Eyes allow Gojo to instantly calculate whether any incoming stimulus
   can breach Infinity and adjust its parameters accordingly in real time.
   Against techniques: Infinity stops technique effects that have physical
   propagation (slashes, blasts, projectiles). Effects that target the soul
   directly (affliction-class) may bypass it.

B) CURSED TECHNIQUE LAPSE: BLUE
   Mechanics: The "negative" application of Limitless. Amplifies the convergence
   property beyond its natural state to create a zone of absolute spatial
   attraction -- a vacuum/singularity point that pulls everything around it
   inward with tremendous force.
   Effect: Creates a point where space collapses inward. Targets near the Blue
   convergence point are pulled violently into it, taking damage from the
   compressed spatial pressure and forced movement.
   Damage type: Physical/Energy (spatial compression force; the pull itself
   does not distinguish between material types)
   Debuff: Can trap or immobilize targets caught in the pull -- effectively a
   stun or positional lockdown
   Cooldown: Moderate (requires energy to amplify beyond passive Infinity level)
   Unique property: The pull affects everything uniformly including non-living
   matter. Highly environmental -- can collapse structures, pull debris as
   shrapnel, and displace multiple targets simultaneously.

C) CURSED TECHNIQUE REVERSAL: RED
   Mechanics: The "positive" (reversed) application, achieved by inverting
   Limitless's negative cursed energy into positive energy. Creates the exact
   opposite of Blue -- a point of absolute repulsion rather than attraction.
   Effect: A blast of explosive repulsive force radiating from a single point.
   Whatever the Red convergence point contacts is launched away with enormous
   kinetic force.
   Damage type: Physical/Energy (kinetic repulsion force)
   Debuff: Extreme knockback -- targets are launched, losing positioning and
   potentially being slammed into walls/terrain for additional impact damage
   Unique property: Because it uses positive (reversed) cursed energy, it is
   more energetically costly than Blue but deals more direct forceful damage.
   Red can also function as a healing method if directed inward (when used as
   Reverse Cursed Technique on Gojo himself). The repulsion is explosive --
   unlike Blue's sustained pull, Red is an instantaneous detonation.

D) HOLLOW TECHNIQUE: PURPLE (Imaginary Technique: Hollow Purple)
   Mechanics: The combination of Blue and Red applied simultaneously and merged.
   The convergence of attraction and repulsion creates an "imaginary" mass that
   does not exist in nature. This imaginary mass "erases" everything it passes
   through -- matter, cursed energy constructs, and living beings contacted by
   Purple are simply deleted from existence as it passes.
   Effect: A sphere or beam of imaginary mass. It does not explode, cut, or
   burn. It erases. Structures struck by Purple have a clean void left where
   the mass passed. Sorcerers and cursed spirits struck are removed.
   Damage type: Affliction (the erasure bypasses all conventional defenses --
   you cannot block something composed of imaginary mass)
   Debuff: None -- targets erased by Purple leave no target to debuff
   Unique property: Purple is the only technique Gojo cannot use while Infinity
   is active. The concurrent generation of Blue and Red destabilizes his own
   spatial field, so he must consciously deactivate Infinity to fire Purple.
   This creates a brief window of vulnerability. Purple travels in a straight
   line and cannot be redirected after firing.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SIX EYES (Rikugan)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Not a technique -- an innate ocular ability that accompanies
Limitless. Only one person per generation can possess both Six Eyes and
Limitless simultaneously (they appear together in the Gojo clan bloodline).
Capabilities:
- See cursed energy at maximum resolution (precise measurement of any
  sorcerer's output, reserves, and technique signatures)
- Read technique signatures in real time -- identifies any technique the
  moment it is initiated
- Map opponent's cursed energy pathways (allows Gojo to predict how an
  attack will propagate)
- Calculate optimal Infinity parameters instantly -- adjusts what Infinity
  blocks or allows through with zero lag
- Perceive truth about cursed techniques at a depth no other sorcerer can match
Practical effect in combat: Near-zero cursed energy waste running Infinity.
The Six Eyes optimize every application so efficiently that Infinity costs
Gojo almost nothing to maintain passively. Any other sorcerer attempting to
run a comparable always-on defense would exhaust themselves in minutes.
Damage type: N/A -- perception ability
Debuff: N/A
Unique property: The Six Eyes are why Gojo can run Infinity constantly.
Without them, Limitless would be too costly to use as a passive defense.
The Six Eyes also make Gojo essentially impossible to surprise -- he sees
technique activations before they manifest.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DOMAIN EXPANSION: UNLIMITED VOID (Muryokusho)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DETAILED MANGA MECHANICS:
Unlimited Void creates a bounded space that represents the interior of true
Infinity -- not a defensive field, but an experiential space that exposes
targets to the fundamental nature of Gojo's technique.

What happens to targets inside:
Anyone caught inside is exposed to infinite simultaneous information: every
piece of knowledge, sensation, data, and stimulus in existence hits their
consciousness all at once, continuously. The effect is not pain or fire or
a cutting force. The victim's mind cannot process infinite concurrent input,
so it processes effectively none of it. The brain locks up trying to handle
unbounded simultaneous stimuli -- targets can perceive everything happening
around them but their nervous system cannot issue any motor commands or make
any decisions. They are fully conscious but completely paralyzed.

Duration effects:
- Sustained exposure of 0.2 seconds real time: Leaves a sorcerer incapacitated
  for days or weeks after leaving the domain. Their nervous system remains
  overloaded long after the domain closes.
- Brief exposure (less than 0.2 sec): Immediate full-body paralysis while
  inside; recovery is faster but still significant.

Effect type: Full stun (while inside) + lasting weaken/nervous system debuff
(persisting after the domain ends)

The sure-hit property: Like all fully realized domains, the technique inside
guarantees contact. The information flood cannot be dodged or physically blocked
because it is not a physical attack -- it is pure experiential stimulus
delivered directly to the mind. There is no moving out of the way of
omnipresent infinite information.

Does not deal traditional damage: The incapacitation is the primary weapon.
A sorcerer paralyzed inside Unlimited Void is defenseless against Gojo
attacking them conventionally while they are locked up.

Against other domain users: If two domains clash, the stronger (more refined/
denser) domain overwrites the other. Unlimited Void is considered essentially
unbeatable in domain clash until the Sukuna fight.

3. MEGUMI FUSHIGURO -- TEN SHADOWS TECHNIQUE
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
The Ten Shadows Technique (Jushiki no Jutsu) allows the user to summon
shikigami using cursed energy channeled through shadows. The user must tame
each shikigami through a ritual combat -- if they lose, the shikigami is
permanently destroyed. Once tamed, each is a distinct entity with unique
abilities. Megumi can use shadows as a medium for partial shikigami constructs
and shadow clones even outside of full summons.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHIKIGAMI: DIVINE DOGS (Byakuya -- White / Kokuyou -- Black)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Two wolf-like shikigami, one white and one black.
Effect: Physical attack shikigami. They chase and bite targets. They are
especially effective against cursed spirits (bonus tracking/damage vs. curses).
Damage type: Physical (fang/claw attacks)
Debuff: None
Unique property: They track cursed spirits relentlessly using a form of
cursed energy scent-tracking. Against sorcerers they function as standard
physical attackers. They can act independently once summoned.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHIKIGAMI: DIVINE DOGS: TOTALITY
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Unlocked condition: One Divine Dog must be killed in combat first.
Mechanics: The surviving dog absorbs the power and essence of the dead one.
It grows massively in size and strength -- larger body, greater speed,
significantly enhanced durability and attack power.
Effect: A single, substantially more powerful wolf that supersedes both
original Divine Dogs.
Damage type: Physical (enhanced fang/claw -- stronger than either original)
Debuff: None
Unique property: This is a permanent upgrade path, not a temporary mode.
Totality is what Megumi deploys after losing one dog in his early battles.
The transformation is irreversible -- once merged, they cannot revert.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHIKIGAMI: NUE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
A chimeric owl-like shikigami with enormous wingspan.
Effect: Aerial attacker. Dives at high speed toward targets and delivers an
electric thunderclap shock on impact.
Damage type: Energy (lightning/electric discharge)
Debuff: The electric shock can stun/briefly paralyze the target (shown in the
anime -- targets hit visibly spasm from the electric discharge). Short duration.
Unique property: Nue can be fused with Toad to create a combined entity.
Nue also provides aerial reconnaissance and can attack at range by swooping,
making it useful against opponents who are strong at close range.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHIKIGAMI: GREAT SERPENT (Orochi)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
An enormous serpent shikigami of massive scale.
Effect: Primarily used to restrain, bind, or block. Megumi summons it to
ensnare enemies, create terrain obstacles, or cut off escape routes.
Damage type: Physical (constriction/crushing if it coils around a target)
Debuff: Restraint/bind -- enemies ensnared by the serpent cannot move freely.
Functions as a stun/immobilize effect.
Unique property: Primary value is control and area denial rather than raw
damage. Megumi frequently uses it to hold an opponent in place while delivering
a follow-up attack. The serpent can also be used to block incoming attacks.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHIKIGAMI: TOAD (Gama)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
A large toad shikigami.
Effect: Defensive/utility. Can swallow allies to teleport them away from
danger (the ally emerges from a different shadow location). Can also swallow
projectiles to absorb/negate incoming ranged attacks.
Damage type: None -- purely defensive utility
Debuff: None
Unique property: When fused with Nue, creates "Max Nue" -- a chimeric
owl-toad hybrid. Max Nue can grab and abduct targets mid-air, transporting
them to a different location (offensive repositioning: throw enemies into
hazards or separate them from allies).

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHIKIGAMI: MAX ELEPHANT (Bai)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
A massive elephant shikigami of enormous scale.
Effect: Summons a torrent of high-pressure water from its trunk, dealing
massive area damage and terrain destruction in a wide cone or stream.
Damage type: Physical (high-pressure water impact/blunt force)
Debuff: Knockback from the water torrent; terrain destruction can forcibly
displace enemies and destroy cover/structures
Unique property: Enormous cursed energy cost. One of Megumi's most draining
summons. Used for area clearing or against entrenched opponents. The water
volume is extreme -- enough to flood enclosed spaces rapidly.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHIKIGAMI: RABBIT ESCAPE (Usagi Escape)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Summons a massive swarm of small rabbit shikigami.
Effect: Evasion and distraction utility. The rabbits overwhelm the area,
occupying and confusing enemies while Megumi repositions or retreats.
Damage type: None -- the rabbits do not deal meaningful damage
Debuff: None
Unique property: This is explicitly NOT a combat technique. It is a tactical
withdrawal and evasion tool. The swarm creates visual chaos and forces enemies
to deal with hundreds of small targets, buying Megumi time to escape or reset
his positioning.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHIKIGAMI: EIGHT-HANDLED SWORD DIVERGENT SILA DIVINE GENERAL MAHORAGA
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
The most powerful shikigami in the Ten Shadows system.
Taming status: UNTAMEABLE. No Ten Shadows user in history has ever
successfully tamed Mahoraga. Megumi cannot control it.
Summoning method: An exorcism ritual (the Summoning Bell). Once the ritual
begins, Mahoraga is released and attacks indiscriminately -- it has no allegiance
to the summoner.
Effect: Extreme physical combat. Mahoraga fights at a level capable of
challenging Special Grade threats. It attacks everything in range.
Damage type: Physical (sword strikes from its massive blade)

THE WHEEL OF ADAPTATION:
Mahoraga has a spinning eight-handled sword wheel above its head. This wheel
represents its adaptation ability. Each time Mahoraga receives a type of damage
or encounters a technique it has not experienced before, the wheel spins.
After sufficient rotations, Mahoraga fully adapts and becomes immune or
resistant to that specific stimulus, technique, or attack type.
- Adaptation extends to its offensive weapon: Mahoraga's sword eventually adapts
  to cut through ANY defense, including Gojo's Infinity (shown in the manga --
  an adapted Mahoraga's blade physically cuts through Infinity)
- Adaptation to Gojo's Infinity is what makes Sukuna's use of Mahoraga
  (in Megumi's body) so devastating against Gojo
Debuff: None applied by Mahoraga to opponents (it simply attacks)
Unique property: Megumi uses Mahoraga as a last resort or self-sacrifice move --
he summons it to kill himself and his opponent simultaneously, relying on his
own survival instinct or a collaborator to manage the aftermath. Sukuna (while
possessing Megumi) weaponized Mahoraga by using his own technique variety to
prevent full adaptation, then using World Slash (Cleave applied to the entire
surrounding environment) to destroy it after it had adapted to Gojo's Infinity.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DOMAIN EXPANSION: CHIMERA SHADOW GARDEN (Incomplete)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
INCOMPLETE vs. COMPLETE DOMAIN -- DETAILED EXPLANATION:

A COMPLETE domain creates a bounded space with a sure-hit technique effect
inside. Any target inside cannot avoid the guaranteed contact with the
domain's interior technique. A complete Chimera Shadow Garden would
presumably fill a space with infinite shadow constructs guaranteeing all
of Megumi's shikigami always make contact with targets.

MEGUMI'S DOMAIN IS INCOMPLETE -- it lacks the sure-hit interior effect.

What the incomplete domain DOES:
1. Creates a bounded shadow-filled space
2. Massively amplifies the Ten Shadows Technique within the space:
   - Shikigami can be summoned at reduced cursed energy cost
   - Shadow constructs and clones can be generated from the abundant shadow
     material permeating the space
   - Partial shikigami can be created without full summoning cost
3. Shadows can be used to obscure positioning, create decoy shadow-clones
   of Megumi, and generate terrain from shadow material
4. Because it lacks the sure-hit, a skilled opponent can still evade
   attacks from Megumi's shikigami within it

STRATEGIC ADVANTAGE of the incomplete domain:
Megumi uses Chimera Shadow Garden as an amplification and information-asymmetry
tool rather than a kill chamber. He has perfect awareness of every shadow in
the space; his opponents do not. He can attack from unexpected angles using the
shadow network simultaneously with physical and shikigami pressure.

4. YUJI ITADORI
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Yuji Itadori is physically gifted to a superhuman degree even before cursed
energy training -- his baseline strength, speed, and durability are far beyond
a normal human. As Sukuna's vessel he absorbs aspects of Sukuna's power
throughout the series, culminating in the revelation that he has inherited
Sukuna's innate cursed technique as his own.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DIVERGENT FIST
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Yuji's innate issue early in the series -- his body was not trained
to synchronize cursed energy release with physical impact. Instead his energy
comes out in two waves: the physical fist impact, then a delayed second burst
of cursed energy hitting a fraction of a second after. This "divergence" means
opponents who reinforce with cursed energy against the first hit are caught
off-guard by the secondary energy wave before they release their reinforcement.
Damage type: Physical (first hit) + Energy (delayed second cursed energy burst)
Debuff: None directly -- the value is the double-impact damage application
Unique property: As Yuji improves cursed energy control, the divergence
diminishes. However, in the final arc he uses Divergent Fist intentionally as
a refined tool where the delayed energy wave is timed to hit the SOUL separately
from the body -- a significant upgrade over his early uncontrolled version.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
BLACK FLASH (Kokusen)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: A technique any sorcerer can theoretically use but almost none can
do on command. Black Flash requires applying cursed energy to a strike within
0.000001 seconds of physical impact. When timed correctly, it creates a spatial
distortion at the point of contact -- a visible black flash of warped space.
The resulting strike deals 2.5 times the normal damage of that hit (this
multiplier is explicitly stated in the manga).
Damage type: Physical + Energy (spatial distortion at impact; 2.5x multiplier
applies to the total output)
Debuff: None, but the power spike can break through cursed energy reinforcement
that would normally withstand the base strike
Post-Black Flash state: After landing Black Flash, the sorcerer enters a
heightened state of cursed energy sensitivity. For a period after, they can
feel their cursed energy with far greater precision, making subsequent Black
Flashes easier to land. This heightened state also opens access to the core
concepts of cursed energy. This is the mechanism by which Yuji went from
struggling with basic control to understanding his inherited technique -- he
chained multiple Black Flashes and entered the heightened state.
Unique property: Yuji is exceptional at Black Flash. He can chain them more
reliably than almost any other sorcerer in the series.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHRINE (Innate Cursed Technique -- Sukuna's CT inherited by Yuji)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
MAJOR REVELATION (late manga / Shinjuku arc):
Yuji does not merely contain Sukuna. He has inherited Sukuna's innate cursed
technique -- Shrine -- as his own personal technique. This was confirmed in the
Shinjuku arc. The technique manifests naturally from Yuji himself, completely
independent of Sukuna's presence.

CLEAVE:
Mechanics: A single adaptive slash of cursed energy. The cut automatically
adjusts its output to match the target's durability and cursed energy level.
Against an uncursed human: instantly lethal. Against a Grade 1 sorcerer:
scales proportionally to require multiple exchanges or one devastating blow.
Damage type: Affliction-class (cursed technique slash; not a physical cutting
action -- bypasses conventional physical defenses)
Debuff: None -- pure scaling damage
Unique property: The adaptive scaling makes Cleave equally dangerous against
any tier of opponent. It cannot be tanked by simply being durable.

DISMANTLE:
Mechanics: Fixed-output barrage of small cursed energy slashes. Not adaptive --
the total power is set regardless of target strength. Multiple cuts fan outward
in a spread pattern. Used against multiple targets or to overwhelm defenses
through volume of smaller cuts rather than one powerful slash.
Damage type: Affliction-class (cursed technique cutting damage, fixed output)
Debuff: None -- pure fixed damage
Unique property: More controllable and versatile than Cleave. More useful for
multi-target situations. Less devastating against single hardened opponents.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SOUL-MANIPULATION / SOUL PUNCHES (Shinjuku / Final Arc)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Yuji discovers he can imbue his physical strikes with the ability
to damage the soul directly -- not just the body. This is a result of his
extended time as Sukuna's vessel (absorbing aspects of Shrine's deeper nature).
His punches become soul-attacks in addition to physical impacts.
Effect: Physical strikes deal damage to the opponent's actual soul, not just
their physical body. Healed flesh does not fully negate soul damage -- the
victim's soul accumulates damage independently of body healing.
Damage type: Affliction (soul damage -- bypasses physical defenses and RCT
healing of physical wounds since the soul is not the body)
Debuff: Cumulative soul damage weakens the opponent even if they continuously
heal their body
Unique property: This makes Yuji one of the few sorcerers whose physical
attacks cannot simply be healed away. A target using RCT to fix body wounds
still accumulates soul damage from his strikes.

5. NOBARA KUGISAKI -- STRAW DOLL TECHNIQUE (Wara Ningyo no Jutsu)
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Nobara's technique connects cursed objects (nails, straw dolls) to targets
through cursed energy. Once a connection is established, damage to the
corresponding doll/nail transmits to the target's soul. This allows her to
damage opponents who are otherwise physically unreachable, invulnerable, or
heavily reinforced.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
NAILS (Basic Technique)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Nobara uses a hammer to drive cursed-energy-infused nails into
targets directly or into straw dolls connected to targets. When a nail is
physically driven into a target, cursed energy is embedded into their body,
creating an energy link.
Damage type: Physical (nail impact) + Energy (cursed energy infusion on impact)
Debuff: The embedded nail creates a connection that subsequent techniques
(Hairpin, Resonance) can exploit for amplified follow-up damage
Unique property: Nails can be thrown as ranged projectiles (driven by hammer
or tossed) or used in melee with the hammer directly.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
HAIRPIN (Kanzashi)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Nobara snaps her fingers -- the "Hairpin" gesture -- to detonate
the cursed energy stored in previously embedded nails. The stored energy
explodes outward from the nail's position at the point of embedding.
Damage type: Energy (cursed energy explosion at the nail site)
Debuff: None directly -- pure detonation damage payoff
Unique property: Requires a nail to be embedded in the target first (combo
setup prerequisite). The explosion damage is proportional to how much cursed
energy was charged into the nail. Can be used to detonate multiple nails
simultaneously for area or multi-target explosions.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
RESONANCE (Kyoumei)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Nobara's defining technique. She drives a nail into her straw doll
(which is connected to a target) and by the curse connection, the damage
transmits directly to the soul of the linked target. The damage bypasses
physical armor, cursed energy reinforcement, invulnerability barriers, and
defensive techniques because it targets the soul, not the body.
Damage type: Affliction (soul damage -- bypasses ALL conventional defenses)
Debuff: None beyond the soul damage itself
Unique property: This is what makes Nobara exceptional against otherwise
durable opponents. Physical toughness and energy reinforcement are irrelevant
to Resonance. She can damage a target who is physically untouchable simply
by striking the doll. Works at range -- she does not need to be near the target
at the moment of the Resonance strike.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SUPERNOVA
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Nobara drives a nail through her own cheek/face. This act of
self-harm and the cursed energy generated by her own pain is used as a
catalyst to amplify the Resonance effect to a maximum level -- a massive
burst of soul-targeting cursed energy transmitted through the doll connection.
Damage type: Affliction (amplified soul damage -- her most powerful output)
Debuff: Nobara takes significant self-damage from the nail through her face
Unique property: Her most powerful single technique, reserved as a last resort.
The self-harm serves as both the catalyst and the Binding Vow mechanism --
by paying a physical price on her own soul/body, she amplifies the transmitted
soul damage exponentially.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DOMAIN EXPANSION: NONE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nobara Kugisaki does not possess a Domain Expansion in the manga.
She is a Grade 3 sorcerer (whose practical ability exceeds her grade) but
does not develop a domain during the series. Her combat identity is built
entirely around the Straw Doll Technique and its soul-damage applications.

6. KENTO NANAMI -- RATIO TECHNIQUE
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Kento Nanami is a Grade 1 sorcerer who operates as a professional. His
Ratio Technique creates a universal mandatory weak point that bypasses the
usual advantage cursed energy reinforcement gives to durable opponents.
He fights with a bandaged cleaver blunt weapon (not a sharpened blade).

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
RATIO TECHNIQUE: 7:3 (Shichi-san no Kotowari)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Nanami divides any target object into a 7:3 ratio. The point at
the exact 7:3 position along any axis of the target becomes a mandatory weak
point -- a location where cursed energy structurally fails to maintain
reinforcement. It is like forcing a structural fault line into any object.
By striking this precise spot, Nanami ensures the target takes full amplified
damage regardless of their cursed energy reinforcement elsewhere.
Damage type: Physical (the strike is physical; the weak-point forces the
cursed energy reinforcement to fail there, effectively bypassing DR that
reinforcement would normally provide -- functionally piercing)
Debuff: None -- the technique creates the weak point automatically on any
object/person and Nanami locates and strikes it
Unique property: Works on anything with an identifiable physical shape --
people, buildings, cursed spirits, objects. The 7:3 ratio creates a universal
structural fault applicable to any material. Nanami identifies the 7:3 point
visually and tactilely, then strikes it with his cleaver.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
COLLAPSE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Nanami applies his Ratio Technique to a large structure (wall,
floor, ceiling, pillar) and strikes the 7:3 point of the structure, causing
catastrophic structural failure. The collapsing structure deals area damage
to everyone in the debris zone.
Damage type: Physical (falling debris, structural collapse, impact force)
Debuff: Area knockdown; terrain destruction displaces enemies and destroys cover
Unique property: Environmentally dependent -- requires a usable structure.
Highly effective in indoor or urban combat. Against opponents in open fields
it cannot be used. Against multiple opponents clustered near a structure,
Collapse is one of his most efficient AoE options.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
OVERTIME (Jikan-gai)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Not a cursed technique in the traditional sense -- a Binding Vow
(Jujutsu contract) Nanami made with his own cursed energy. During defined
"work hours" he caps his cursed energy output artificially as a sacrifice.
The compensation for this restriction is that after work hours (or when he
explicitly invokes "Overtime"), the cap lifts entirely and his cursed energy
output dramatically increases, proportionally boosting all technique damage.
Damage type: N/A -- self-buff (power ceiling removal)
Debuff: None applied to enemies
Unique property: The Binding Vow mechanism means his power ceiling is
artificially suppressed most of the time. Overtime removes this cap. His
Ratio Technique damage under Overtime is substantially higher than standard
output. Nanami treats this as a literal work/life separation -- he intentionally
does not use his full power during defined work hours as part of his philosophy.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DOMAIN EXPANSION: NONE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
No confirmed Domain Expansion for Kento Nanami in the manga. He never uses one.
Nanami is a fundamentals-based sorcerer who relies entirely on Ratio Technique
mastery, physical combat skill, and tactical intelligence rather than a domain.

7. HAKARI KINJI -- IDLE DEATH GAMBLE
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Hakari Kinji is a student at Tokyo Jujutsu High who was suspended for fighting
with higher-ups. He has enormous cursed energy reserves described as among the
highest of his generation. His cursed energy is "passionate" and unstable --
his Domain reflects his personality: a gambling machine that offers near-
immortality as the jackpot.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DOMAIN EXPANSION: IDLE DEATH GAMBLE (Shinjitsu Issun Koumyou Issen)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hakari's entire combat identity is built around this domain. He deploys it
actively in combat and it runs continuously while active.

THE PACHINKO MACHINE:
Inside the domain, a Pachinko game plays out. The domain's interior logic
follows the plot of a real doujin manga called "Ding-Dong! Dash" -- a
romantic comedy that Hakari is obsessed with. The domain recreates the
manga's setting and runs through its story. The Pachinko machine's balls
represent the cursed energy cycle of the domain.

JACKPOT MECHANICS (detailed):
- Each round, the Pachinko machine runs through its trajectory
- The Jackpot is determined by the manga's plot resolution -- the romantic
  climax of "Ding-Dong! Dash" playing out successfully within the domain
- When Jackpot triggers: Hakari's Reverse Cursed Technique activates
  continuously at MAXIMUM output for approximately 4 minutes
- During Jackpot: Hakari's body regenerates in real time at a rate that
  makes him functionally immortal. ANY damage done to him is reversed:
    * Dismemberment: limbs reattach or regrow
    * Lethal strikes through vital organs: organs regenerate within seconds
    * Complete destruction of body parts: reversed within the window
  He cannot be killed during the Jackpot window by conventional means.
- After Jackpot expires (approximately 4 minutes): domain resets and the
  machine runs again for a new round
- Hakari can re-deploy the domain to start a new round

NON-JACKPOT COMBAT:
Outside of Jackpot, Hakari is an exceptional physical brawler. He fights
with powerful strikes enhanced by his enormous cursed energy reinforcement.
His cursed energy output is high enough that even without Jackpot he is
a Grade 1 level threat through raw power alone.

THE NEAR-IMMORTALITY IN COMBAT -- PRACTICAL DESCRIPTION:
Fighting Hakari during Jackpot:
- Opponents must survive for the full 4-minute Jackpot window (extremely
  difficult given he brawls recklessly knowing he cannot die)
- OR find a way to deal damage that RCT cannot reverse:
    * Soul damage (affliction-class techniques targeting the soul directly)
    * Cursed technique-level destruction that overwhelms the RCT rate
    * Techniques that prevent RCT from functioning
- Hakari's strategy: enter domain, force Jackpot, then brawl with zero
  self-preservation instinct because he genuinely cannot die during the window

SURE-HIT PROPERTY:
Inside Idle Death Gamble's active space, the sure-hit applies the Pachinko
narrative -- the domain forces the internal game to run on all targets inside.
The "hit" is not a damage technique but the imposition of the Jackpot cycle
on the battlefield. This is an unusual domain whose guaranteed interior
effect is a condition (the gambling mechanic) rather than direct damage.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
RESTLESS LOVE (Physical Brawling)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Hakari's raw physical striking -- powerful melee attacks enhanced
by cursed energy reinforcement. He drains the opponent through sustained
aggressive combat.
Damage type: Physical
Unique property: He intentionally absorbs hits to maintain close range and
pressure. His fighting style is designed for attrition -- he bets on outlasting
opponents through his healing mechanics.

8. PANDA -- THREE CORES
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Panda is an "Abrupt Mutated Cursed Corpse" -- not a real panda, but a unique
autonomous cursed corpse created by Principal Masamichi Yaga. What makes Panda
exceptional is that Yaga built THREE different "souls" (cores) inside him,
each with a different personality and ability set. Most cursed corpses have
one core/soul; Panda has three.

CORE MECHANICS:
Each core is a distinct soul entity housed within Panda's body. In combat,
Panda can switch which core is active, altering his capabilities. When one
core takes too much damage, it "retreats" (goes dormant to recover) and Panda
can switch to another. Each core has its own cursed energy reservoir.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
PANDA CORE (Default / "Younger Brother")
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
The "Panda" personality and default fighting mode.
Effect: Standard balanced melee combat. This is Panda's casual/default state.
Damage type: Physical (punches, physical strikes)
Debuff: None specifically
Unique property: Acts as the neutral baseline between the two specialized
cores. This core represents Panda's everyday personality -- talkative,
friendly, observational. In combat it is competent but not exceptional.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
GORILLA CORE ("Older Brother")
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Activated when Panda switches to his second soul.
In Gorilla mode, Panda's physical power increases dramatically. His body
structure and fighting style shifts to optimize for raw overwhelming force.

UNBLOCKABLE DRUMMING BEAT:
Mechanics: Rapid double-palm strike executed simultaneously from both arms.
The mechanical key: the attack comes from both sides at exactly the same time.
Blocking one arm means the other connects. Blocking both means no guard is
possible. The strike is designed around the physical impossibility of fully
blocking a simultaneous bilateral attack -- hence "Unblockable."
Damage type: Physical (blunt force impact)
Debuff: None -- pure damage
Unique property: Mechanically defeats conventional guard/block techniques.
Any opponent who raises their arms to guard is hit by the unblocked side.
Any opponent who does not guard is hit by both. The only answer is complete
evasion, not blocking. This is the Gorilla core's signature technique.

GORILLA BOOST:
General strength amplification while Gorilla core is active. All strikes
deal more damage and Panda's body is more durable/impact-resistant.
Damage type: N/A (self-buff)
Effect: Increases all physical damage output; increases body durability

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
TRIDENT CORE ("Older Sister")
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Activated when Panda switches to his third soul.
The Trident core is focused on sustain and recovery rather than offense.
Effect: Allows Panda to redirect cursed energy from his storage cores into
self-healing/regeneration. He restores damaged flesh, seals wounds, and
replenishes stamina.
Damage type: N/A -- healing/recovery
Debuff: None
Unique property: Panda's three-core system means he effectively has three
separate HP pools. When one core is critically damaged, he switches and
the damaged core slowly recovers dormant while another fights. This gives
him a de facto second and third "life" in extended combat, making him
substantially harder to permanently kill than a standard cursed corpse.

LOSING CORES:
If a core is fully destroyed (not just damaged), it is permanently gone.
Panda has lost cores during the series. A Panda with all three cores is at
full strength; losing one reduces his options, stamina ceiling, and versatility.
A Panda with only one core remaining fights with all the desperation of someone
who knows the next defeat is final.

9. TOGE INUMAKI -- CURSED SPEECH (Jujutsu: Kotodama)
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Toge Inumaki is a descendant of the Inumaki clan, which developed a unique
lineage technique: applying cursed energy to spoken words, forcing reality
to comply with their literal meaning. The technique is marked by the "Snake
Eyes and Fangs" inscription visible on his face (around his mouth, cheeks,
and throat).

SELF-DAMAGE MECHANIC:
Using Cursed Speech damages Inumaki's own throat -- the cursed energy backlash
from forcing commands on resistant targets (especially strong sorcerers or
special-grade curses) causes internal damage to his vocal cords. The stronger
the target's resistance, the more damage Inumaki takes to force the command
through. This is why he communicates in food ingredient words in casual
conversation -- to avoid accidentally activating the technique on bystanders.

POWER SCALING:
The effectiveness of each command is proportional to:
1. Inumaki's cursed energy output at the time of speaking
2. The target's cursed energy level (resistance)
3. The severity of the command (higher stakes commands cost more to force through)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMPLETE COMMAND LIST WITH EFFECTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

DON'T MOVE (Ugokunna)
Effect: Forces the target's body to halt all voluntary movement. Full-body
paralysis -- the target cannot take any physical action.
Damage type: None -- pure control/stun
Duration: Until cursed energy dissipates or target actively resists
Resistance: Strong sorcerers can push through at the cost of internal injury
Use pattern: His most-used command. Reliable control against most targets.

STOP (Tomare)
Effect: Similar to "Don't Move" but applied to objects and momentum as well
as living beings. Stops all motion. Can halt mid-air projectiles, falling
objects, and moving people simultaneously.
Damage type: None -- control
Unique property: Environmental utility beyond "Don't Move" -- can stop
physical projectiles and falling objects, not only living targets.

BLAST AWAY (Tobichire)
Effect: Commands the target to fly away/be expelled violently. Extreme
knockback -- targets are launched with tremendous force.
Damage type: Physical (kinetic -- the force of being launched causes impact
damage upon landing or collision with terrain/obstacles)
Debuff: Extreme positional displacement; can be used to scatter groups
Unique property: Most commonly shown offensive command. Used against large
swarms of cursed spirits. One of his primary damage-dealing commands.

PLUMMET (Otore)
Effect: Commands the target to fall/drop with sudden gravitational force.
Used against aerial targets to forcibly ground them, or against standing
targets to slam them downward suddenly.
Damage type: Physical (impact from forced fall)
Debuff: Grounds aerial opponents; causes staggering on impact; disrupts
any in-progress aerial attacks or repositioning

SLEEP (Nemure)
Effect: Commands the target to fall unconscious. Gentler than paralysis --
the target loses consciousness rather than being frozen in place.
Damage type: None -- incapacitation
Unique property: Works well against weaker opponents. Strong sorcerers and
special-grade curses can resist sleep more easily than movement commands.
Tactically useful for non-lethal capture or reducing collateral risk.

EXPLODE (Haretsu)
Effect: Commands the target's body to rupture and explode from within.
Internal detonation of the target's biology -- catastrophic internal damage
from the inside out.
Damage type: Affliction (internal body destruction; bypasses surface-level
physical defenses and cursed energy reinforcement since the explosion
originates from within the body)
Unique property: One of his most powerful single commands. Reserved for
serious threats. The internal detonation mechanism means it cannot be
blocked by external defensive techniques.

TWIST (Nejire)
Effect: Commands the target's body to twist and contort against its natural
alignment. Severe internal structural damage -- bones, muscles, and organs
are forced to rotate and twist catastrophically.
Damage type: Affliction (internal structural destruction)
Unique property: Against strong opponents this is Inumaki's most devastating
weapon. It was the command Yuta borrowed and amplified through a megaphone
in JJK 0. The internal nature of the damage bypasses external defenses
entirely. Even partial compliance with "Twist" causes severe injury.

RUN AWAY (Nigero)
Effect: Compulsion to flee -- forces the target to turn and retreat from
the command source.
Damage type: None -- behavioral control
Unique property: Not a damage command. Used tactically to break an enemy
advance, create separation, or disengage a dangerous opponent without
attempting to incapacitate them.

DIE (Shinde)
Effect: Forces the target to cease living -- theoretically instant death.
Damage type: Affliction (existential/soul-level command to cease living)
Unique property: The most extreme command. In practice it has only been shown
effective against opponents with minimal cursed energy resistance. Against a
full-strength sorcerer or special-grade cursed spirit, the resistance they
generate would likely cause severe damage to Inumaki himself. The trade-off is
extreme -- using "Die" against a resistant target risks Inumaki's own survival.
It is his ultimate emergency command with the highest personal cost.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DOMAIN EXPANSION: NONE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Toge Inumaki does not develop or use a Domain Expansion in the manga.
He relies entirely on Cursed Speech for combat.

10. NORITOSHI KAMO -- BLOOD MANIPULATION (Ketsu Sujuu)
===========================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Noritoshi Kamo is an heir of the Kamo clan, one of the three great jujutsu
families. Blood Manipulation allows him to control the physical properties of
blood -- its flow, speed, viscosity, temperature, shape, and structural state.
He primarily uses his own blood (shed through cuts he makes during combat) but
the technique theoretically applies to any blood.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
BLOOD SWORD (Chiken)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Solidifies blood into blade-shapes or sharp spike projectiles.
Noritoshi bleeds deliberately and solidifies the blood into rigid cutting
edges that he wields like weapons or launches as projectiles.
Effect: Physical cutting damage at close range or ranged piercing projectiles
Damage type: Physical (solidified blood cutting edge)
Debuff: None -- pure physical damage
Unique property: The blood-blades can be wielded like conventional weapons
in melee or launched as thrown projectiles. Solidified blood under cursed
energy reinforcement is substantially stronger than steel.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
CRIMSON BINDING (Akubyou)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Hardens blood into rope-like or web-like structures that surround
and ensnare a target, restraining them physically. The binding coils around
the target's limbs and body.
Effect: Physical restraint and immobilization of the target
Damage type: None directly (the binding itself causes no damage) -- control
Debuff: Full movement restriction equivalent to stun -- target cannot move
freely or execute actions requiring physical mobility while bound
Unique property: Composed of cursed-energy-infused solidified blood, making
it substantially stronger than physical rope or chains. Breaking free requires
significant force or a technique capable of cutting the hardened blood.
The binding can be combined with other techniques -- for example, binding a
target then firing Convergence into the immobilized opponent.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONVERGENCE (Shuuran)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Rapidly compresses blood at extreme speed into a single dense ball
or sphere, then launches it as a high-velocity projectile. The compression
makes the projectile extremely dense, hard, and fast.
Effect: High-speed piercing projectile impact
Damage type: Piercing (the density and launch velocity allow it to punch
through conventional defenses and cursed energy reinforcement; the compressed
ball does not detonate -- it punches through)
Debuff: None -- pure piercing damage
Unique property: Likely the fastest projectile in Noritoshi's toolkit.
The compression produces a projectile harder and denser than any normal material.
Can be combined with Flowing Red Scale (Surge) for amplified output.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
FLOWING RED SCALE (Ryuuketsu no Kizan)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mechanics: Alters the properties of Noritoshi's own blood circulation to
induce a hyper-stimulated combat state. Raises blood temperature, increases
adrenaline delivery, maximizes oxygen transport to muscles, and optimizes
neural response times. Effectively a biological performance-enhancing system.

STACK (Kasane):
Progressive levels of enhancement applied incrementally. Each Stack level
increases the biological stimulation further.
Effect: Speed and power amplification at each Stack level
Damage type: N/A (self-buff)

SURGE (Houdou):
Maximum activation of Flowing Red Scale -- full body enhancement at peak.
Effect: Maximum speed, power, and reaction time amplification
Damage type: N/A (self-buff)

Debuff: None applied to enemies -- self-enhancement only
Unique property: The technique has a physical cost -- his own blood is pushed
past normal biological limits. High Stack or prolonged Surge can damage his
own circulatory system. A physiological Binding Vow: the more enhancement he
draws, the more he risks internal damage to himself.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
EIGHT-HANDLED SWORD DIVERGENT SILA DIVINE GENERAL KUMOKIRI
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Kamo clan's equivalent to the Ten Shadows Technique's Mahoraga --
a blood-aligned divine general shikigami summoned through blood manipulation.
Effect: Summons a powerful divine shikigami that fights with the summoner
Damage type: Physical + Energy (shikigami combat attacks)
Debuff: Blood-based DoT on struck targets (the blood-aligned nature of the
shikigami leaves cursed blood contamination on wounds)
Unique property: Unlike Megumi's Mahoraga which cannot be controlled, Kumokiri
appears to be directable by Noritoshi -- he can command it in combat. This is
Noritoshi's trump card / ultimate technique, deployed only in the most serious
combat situations.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
DOMAIN EXPANSION: NONE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
No confirmed Domain Expansion for Noritoshi Kamo in the manga. He is a
Grade 1 sorcerer with strong Blood Manipulation mastery but does not develop
or deploy a domain during the series.

MEGUMI: 5 skills including Domain. Mahoraga as "summon + invuln" captures the

(AoE water), Rabbit Escape (evasion). These could expand the kit if desired.

NOBARA: characters.py has 4 skills ending at Resonance. Supernova exists in
docs/characters.json but NOT in characters.py -- reconcile this discrepancy.

NANAMI: 4 skills (Ratio 7:3, Collapse, Overtime, Binding Vow). Canon-accurate.
The 7:3 as damage + DoT (15 affliction/t for 2t) is a good mechanical abstraction.

YUTA: 5 skills. Copy as pierce + strengthen is a clever abstraction. Missing
Sukuna slash copies (Cleave/Dismantle) as distinct skills. Domain is thematically
correct. Vector (Dhruv CT) could be added as a counter-trap skill.

HAKARI: 4 skills. Jackpot should perhaps have a near-invuln window to reflect
the 4-minute immortality -- currently modeled as afflict + heal which captures
the concept but understates the invulnerability aspect.

PANDA: 4 skills (Drum Beat AoE, Gorilla Unblockable, Gorilla Boost, Trident
Regen). Canon-accurate and well-designed. Panda Core baseline is implied as
default state before switching.

INUMAKI: 4 skills (Don't Move stun, Blast Away damage, Explode afflict, Throat
Medicine self-heal). Missing: Twist (most powerful command; the one Yuta copies),
Plummet, Sleep, Stop, Run Away, Die. Adding Twist as a distinct skill would
meaningfully differentiate Inumaki from Yuta's "Copy: Cursed Speech."

NORITOSHI: 4 skills (Convergence pierce, Crimson Binding stun+DoT, Flowing Red
Scale Surge, Kumokiri). Missing: Blood Sword (basic physical), Stack variant of
Flowing Red Scale (progressive buff). The current kit is functional but could
add a base attack and the Stack setup into the skill lineup.
