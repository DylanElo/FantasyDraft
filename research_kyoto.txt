1. AOI TODO
==================================================
Grade: Special Grade (self-assessed); officially Grade 1
School: Kyoto Jujutsu High, 3rd year
Notable: One of strongest sorcerers of his generation. Lost left arm during Shibuya Incident.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: BOOGIE WOOGIE ---
Activation Trigger: Clapping his hands together
Core Mechanic:
  - Swaps the positions of any two objects or people that have cursed energy within range
  - The swap is instantaneous - functions as spatial transposition
  - No visible warning or tell before the swap occurs

Targeting Options:
  - Himself with an enemy
  - An enemy with a third party or object
  - Any two cursed-energy-bearing entities within range

Key Properties:
  - Extremely hard to read; any clap triggers it
  - Can trigger with one hand (after losing left arm: claps prosthetic or stump against thigh/chest)
  - No line of sight required once target is designated
  - Range: undefined hard cap; shown at minimum to work across combat engagement range
  - Objects/people WITHOUT cursed energy CANNOT be swapped (major limitation)
  - Can chain multiple swaps in rapid succession
  - Damage Type: Positional/spatial - no direct damage; creates openings for physical strikes

Post-Shibuya Adaptation:
  - After losing his left arm, Todo adapted trigger to his HEARTBEAT
  - Internalizes the "clap" as a rhythmic pulse; no external motion to read
  - Makes the technique even harder to predict and counter

Tactical Depth:
  - Primary danger is disorientation and rhythm-breaking, not raw power
  - Breaks enemy positioning, repositions Todo into attack range
  - Can pull allies out of danger
  - Combined with Itadori: enables near-simultaneous multi-directional strikes

--- TECHNIQUE: IMAGINATION OF LOVING YOUR BEST FRIEND ---
Also called: "Thinking About Your Best Friend" / "BFF Boost"
Type: Self-amplification
Mechanic:
  - Todo temporarily massively amplifies his cursed energy output
  - Triggered by mentally focusing on a specific emotional state
  - Most strongly triggered by imagining meeting/bonding with his "best friend"
  - (Triggered by Takada-chan or Yuji Itadori in practice)
  - This is a REAL, documented in-universe enhancement - not metaphorical
  - Produces genuine spike in cursed energy, similar to emotional unlocking of latent potential
  - Amplification is temporary and contextual
  - Damage Type: Enhances physical output and reinforces Boogie Woogie effectiveness
  - Rule-Breaking Properties: None specific; emotional amplification only

--- PHYSICAL ABILITIES ---
  - Superhuman physical strength even without cursed energy enhancement
  - Black Belt practitioner in martial arts; extensive hand-to-hand training
  - Cursed Energy Reinforcement: standard technique to harden body and enhance strikes
  - Can adapt Boogie Woogie to any clapping motion including one-handed

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
Todo does not possess or use a Domain Expansion at any point in the manga.
Relies entirely on Boogie Woogie positional chaos and overwhelming physical dominance.

2. MAKI ZEN'IN
==================================================
Grade: Started Grade 4 (no CE, couldn't be assessed normally)
      After full Heavenly Restriction awakening: functionally Special Grade or above in physical combat
School: Tokyo Jujutsu High (transferred from Kyoto lineage)
Notable: Twin sister of Mai Zenin. Born into Zenin clan but unable to use cursed energy.

--- INNATE CONDITION: HEAVENLY RESTRICTION (PARTIAL - PRE-MAI'S DEATH) ---
Type: Congenital binding vow applied to the body itself (NOT a cursed technique)
Mechanic: Trades cursed energy for superhuman physical ability
Partial State:
  - Near-zero cursed energy (just barely enough to see cursed spirits)
  - Heightened physical ability above normal human limits (not yet maximum)
  - Required cursed tools to interact with/damage cursed spirits
  - Without CE coating from tools, could not harm spirits directly

--- INNATE CONDITION: HEAVENLY RESTRICTION (FULLY AWAKENED - POST-MAI'S DEATH) ---
Trigger: Mai sacrificed herself; used Construction technique to remove last trace of Maki's CE
Result of Full Awakening:
  - Body became a perfected physical weapon with ZERO cursed energy whatsoever
  - Cannot be sensed by cursed energy detection (no CE presence to detect)
  - Physical body gains properties similar to a cursed tool
  - Can damage and destroy cursed spirits and curse users with BARE HANDS, no weapon needed
  - Speed, strength, durability reached Special Grade threat level
  - Single-handedly dismantled entire Zenin clan main family in this state
  - Body resists CE-based attacks in unusual ways due to total CE absence
  - Damage Type: Pure physical kinetic force
  - Rule-Breaking Property: Zero CE output = essentially INVISIBLE to cursed energy sensing
                            Cannot be tracked by standard sorcerer detection methods

--- WEAPONS AND CURSED TOOLS ---

1. PLAYFUL CLOUD (Special Grade Cursed Tool)
   Type: Three-section staff (sansetsukon)
   Special Grade Designation: One of most powerful non-innate tools in existence
   Properties:
     - Amplifies force behind strikes significantly
     - Difficult to wield properly; requires exceptional physical skill
     - NO special energy-based properties; power is purely physical amplification and speed
   Damage Type: Blunt/impact force; extreme physical striking
   Original User: Toji Fushiguro

2. DRAGON-BONE (Special Grade Cursed Tool)
   Type: Large spear/polearm
   Core Property: ABSORBS force/impact of attacks directed at it; redirects/stores that force
   Mechanics:
     - When Maki blocks or receives a strike with Dragon-Bone, weapon absorbs kinetic energy
     - She can release absorbed force in subsequent attack, adding it to her own output
     - Makes it extremely dangerous to strike her while wielding it - attacking feeds her counterattack
     - Absorption is not unlimited but highly effective within range
   Damage Type: Physical piercing + redirected kinetic energy
   Rule-Breaking: Force absorption turns enemy attacks against them

3. SPLIT SOUL KATANA
   Type: Katana
   Core Property: Cuts the SOUL directly, not just the physical body
   Mechanics:
     - Wounds target the soul layer
     - Damage to the soul is NOT healable by Reverse Cursed Technique
     - RCT heals physical body/CE structure, not the soul itself
   Damage Type: Soul-layer damage; bypasses all physical healing
   Rule-Breaking Property: BYPASSES Reverse Cursed Technique healing - wounds are permanent

4. BARE-HANDED COMBAT (Post-Full Heavenly Restriction)
   - Body itself functions as weapon capable of damaging cursed spirits and sorcerers
   - Can destroy high-grade cursed spirits with her fists
   - Strikes carry force lethal to heavily reinforced targets

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
Cannot use cursed techniques, therefore cannot create a Domain Expansion.
Power is entirely physical.
Compensates with Simple Domain knowledge (conceptual) and raw speed/power to close range.

3. YOSHINOBU GAKUGANJI
==================================================
Grade: Grade 1 Sorcerer
Role: Principal of Kyoto Jujutsu High; Member of Jujutsu Higher-Ups
Notable: Conservative elder who initially supported Yuji's execution. Uses electric guitar.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: CURSED SOUND / ELECTRIC GUITAR TECHNIQUE ---
Common Names: "Tenor Cursed Speech," "Cursed Sound Technique," "Electric Guitar Technique"
No single standardized English name given in manga

Mechanic:
  - Gakuganji infuses his electric guitar with cursed energy and plays it
  - The music/sound produced is imbued with cursed energy
  - Cursed sound waves propagate outward and damage anything in their path
  - Waves damage both the physical body AND the cursed energy structure of targets
  - Can affect multiple targets simultaneously (area of effect/omnidirectional)

Properties:
  - Technique requires active guitar playing (performance-dependent)
  - Sound propagates in all directions from source; can be oriented toward target
  - Sound waves are invisible and difficult to dodge once music begins
  - Higher volume/intensity = higher damage output
  - Affects cursed spirits and humans alike
  - Damage Type: Cursed energy sonic/vibration damage to body and CE structure

Named Sub-Techniques: NONE individually named in the manga
  - The guitar technique is his entire kit; no sub-techniques with separate names
  - Applied with varying intensity rather than distinct named moves

Limitations:
  - Must be actively playing guitar to use
  - Can be disrupted if prevented from playing
  - Optimal at mid-range where sound propagates effectively; less ideal in close combat

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
No Domain Expansion is shown or referenced for Gakuganji.

4. MEI MEI
==================================================
Grade: Grade 1 Sorcerer (Independent/Mercenary)
Notable: Older sister of Ui Ui. Operates for payment only. Uses battle-axe in melee.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: BLACK BIRD MANIPULATION ---
Core Mechanic:
  - Mei Mei controls crows by sharing her consciousness with them
  - Maintains mental link with multiple crows simultaneously
  - Can see through their eyes, direct movement, and use them as combat tools
  - Can implant cursed energy into a crow and direct specific actions

Control Details:
  - Crows act as scouts, surveillance, and combat tools
  - Multiple crows can be active simultaneously
  - Crows act as extensions of her will with high precision

--- TECHNIQUE: BIRD STRIKE ---
Mechanic:
  - Mei Mei concentrates her FULL cursed energy into a single crow
  - The crow performs a suicide dive-bomb on the target
  - Crow's body, saturated with cursed energy, detonates on impact
  - Resulting explosion of cursed energy is extremely powerful

Bypass Property (CRITICAL):
  - Bird Strike can bypass/overwhelm INFINITY
  - Explicitly shown against Pseudo-Geto (Kenjaku) during Shibuya Incident
  - Infinity is proportional to threat - Bird Strike overwhelms proportional threshold
  - Attack is physically small (crow body) but packed with enormous CE concentration
  - Practical result: gets through Infinity
  - Damage Type: Explosive cursed energy detonation at point of impact
  - Rule-Breaking Property: Bypasses Infinity through CE concentration overwhelming proportional defense

Enhanced Bird Strike:
  - Multiple crows charged and released simultaneously or in sequence
  - Used against Smallpox Deity curse (Special Grade) during Shibuya
  - Multiple successive Bird Strikes can destroy a Special Grade curse

Cost: One crow destroyed per use. Supply is not unlimited but Mei Mei commands multiple crows.

--- MERCENARY BINDING VOW ---
Mechanic:
  - Mei Mei genuinely, sincerely fights ONLY for monetary compensation
  - Not just personality - formalized binding vow
  - Cannot act charitably or out of altruism without breaking the vow
  - The genuine personal cost of the vow amplifies her cursed energy output
Effect: Maximizes CE available to Bird Strike; makes her output unusually high for Grade 1
Rule-Breaking: Does not bypass defenses, but amplified CE makes Bird Strike more potent

--- PHYSICAL COMBAT ---
  - Battle-axe (large, cleaver-type weapon) as primary melee weapon
  - Exceptionally strong physical combatant for Grade 1
  - Cursed energy reinforcement applied to axe strikes

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
No Domain Expansion shown or referenced for Mei Mei.

5. NAOBITO ZEN'IN
==================================================
Grade: SPECIAL GRADE (only active Special Grade sorcerer besides Gojo and Yuta at time of appearance)
Role: Head of the Zenin clan
Notable: Father of Naoya Zenin. Severe alcoholic. Fastest sorcerer alive excluding Gojo Satoru.
         Killed during Shibuya Incident by Dagon's Domain Expansion.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: PROJECTION SORCERY ---

Core Mechanic - The 24-Frame Animation Rule:
  - Naobito perceives and divides his movements into 24 frames per second
  - References standard frame rate of hand-drawn animation
  - Plans out movement sequences in these 24 frames in advance ("animating" himself)
  - Once a movement sequence is mapped, executes it at extraordinary speed
  - Faster than the human eye and most CE sensing can track

Effect on Targets He Touches:
  - If Naobito touches a target, he projects that target into the same 24-frame division
  - Target must then complete its movement within those 24 frames
  - If target fails to move in a way fitting 24 discrete frames: FROZEN/IMMOBILIZED
  - A target "outside the frames" is locked in place until sequence resolves
  - This immobilization does not rely on physical restraint; it is motion-based temporal lock
  - Damage Type: Physical strikes enhanced by speed; immobilization
  - Rule-Breaking Property: Temporal/motion-based lock - not CE binding but frame-based paralysis

Practical Speed:
  - 24-frame pre-planned movement makes him fastest non-Infinity sorcerer shown
  - Can cross combat distances in fractions of a second
  - Even experienced fighters struggle to perceive his movement

Limitations:
  - Requires advance planning per movement sequence - cannot improvise mid-sequence
  - Against unpredictable opponents, cognitive cost to replanning exists
  - Alcohol dependence affected peak performance but still Special Grade tier

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
Naobito does not use a Domain Expansion. He is killed by Dagon's Domain Expansion
(Horizon of the Captivating Skandha) before being able to attempt one.
No Domain Expansion referenced as part of his known abilities.

6. TOJI FUSHIGURO
==================================================
Grade: Historically unranked (zero CE prevents assessment). Practically Special Grade or above.
Role: Hired assassin; "Sorcerer Killer"; father of Megumi Fushiguro
Notable: Born Zenin clan. Heavenly Restriction at complete level. All technique use via tools only.

--- INNATE CONDITION: HEAVENLY RESTRICTION (COMPLETE) ---
Properties:
  - ABSOLUTE ZERO cursed energy - cannot be sensed, tracked, or targeted by CE detection
  - Body is perfected physical weapon: superhuman strength, speed, durability, reflexes
  - Exceeds what even trained sorcerers with reinforcement can achieve physically
  - Body "compensates" for zero CE with unparalleled physical attributes
  - Can perceive cursed spirits and sorcerers despite zero CE (extensive experience + heightened senses)
  - Not immune to CE attacks but can survive/power through damage that would destroy others
  - Rule-Breaking Property: Zero CE = COMPLETELY INVISIBLE to CE-based tracking/sensing

--- WEAPONS ARSENAL ---

1. INVERTED SPEAR OF HEAVEN (Special Grade Cursed Tool)
   Type: Thin needle/spike; small, concealable
   Core Property (CRITICAL RULE-BREAKER):
     - Nullifies ALL cursed techniques upon contact
     - ANY cursed technique touching the Inverted Spear is CANCELED/NULLIFIED
     - Includes: Infinity (Gojo's automatic barrier), binding techniques, any technique on surfaces
     - Nullified Gojo's Infinity during their fight - one of the only things that can do this
     - Does not matter how powerful the technique is; Spear nullifies unconditionally
   Limitations:
     - Nullification only applies at point of contact with the spear itself
     - Does not project or spread from the spear
   Damage Type: Physical piercing; technique nullification on contact
   Rule-Breaking Property: UNCONDITIONAL technique nullifier on contact; no exceptions shown

2. PLAYFUL CLOUD (Special Grade Cursed Tool)
   Type: Three-section staff (sansetsukon)
   Key Note: EXPLICITLY has NO cursed technique embedded in it
   Special Grade designation is PURELY due to physical danger and craftsmanship
   No special cursed ability - value is raw striking power
   Toji was the original user who made this weapon famous
   Damage Type: Pure physical impact; massive blunt force from three connected sections

3. SPLIT SOUL KATANA
   Type: Katana
   Core Property: Cuts the SOUL directly, bypassing physical defenses
   Mechanics:
     - Wounds inflicted target the soul layer, not just physical body
     - Damage to soul is NOT healable by Reverse Cursed Technique
     - RCT operates on physical/CE structure, not soul
   Damage Type: Soul-layer damage; permanent wounds bypassing healing
   Rule-Breaking Property: Wounds CANNOT be healed by RCT; bypasses all physical healing

4. UNNAMED ADDITIONAL WEAPONS
   - Swords, chains, and various other cursed tools
   - Retrieved from inventory via Storage Curse (see below)
   - Arsenal is effectively unlimited in variety during combat

--- THE STORAGE CURSE / INVENTORY WORM ---
What It Is:
  - Small, non-combatant cursed spirit stored INSIDE Toji's body (throat/stomach area)
  - Acts as a living inventory for his entire weapons arsenal

Function:
  - Stores all of Toji's weapons within its body
  - Toji retrieves any weapon at any time by reaching into his mouth/throat
  - Can switch weapons mid-combat with zero preparation overhead
  - Enemy cannot disarm him by destroying or taking one weapon

The Worm Itself:
  - Has NO combat ability whatsoever
  - Exclusively a storage solution
  - Not a shikigami; cannot fight

Rule-Breaking Properties:
  - Not a defense bypass; practical implication: Toji is NEVER out of weapons
  - Can present unexpected tools at any moment during combat

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
Cannot use cursed techniques due to Heavenly Restriction.
Cannot create Domain Expansion (requires cursed technique as foundation).
One of the defining trade-offs of complete Heavenly Restriction.

7. YUKI TSUKUMO
==================================================
Grade: SPECIAL GRADE Sorcerer (one of four Special Grade sorcerers in Japan)
Role: Independent sorcerer; researcher on origin of cursed energy
Notable: Mentor figure to Aoi Todo. Fights Kenjaku in late manga arcs.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: STAR RAGE (IMAGINARY MASS) ---
Core Mechanic:
  - Adds "virtual mass" to herself or her shikigami
  - Virtual mass is NOT real physical mass - it is a cursed energy construct
  - Behaves mechanically LIKE mass for purposes of force calculations
  - Result: strikes carry force of an astronomically massive object
  - Her physical body remains the same size; only the force value changes

Self-Application:
  - Adds virtual mass to her own body
  - Punches and strikes hit with force proportional to added virtual mass
  - Even without weapons, can deliver strikes with near-unlimited force (planet-scale implications)
  - Can charge up mass over time for larger strikes

Garuda (Shikigami):
  - Shikigami takes form of large, powerful combat entity
  - Yuki can apply Star Rage virtual mass to Garuda instead of (or in addition to) herself
  - Garuda enhanced with virtual mass becomes overwhelming physical force multiplier
  - Garuda can operate semi-independently while Yuki manages the mass application

Damage Type: Extreme kinetic/impact force; not energy-based but physically amplified crushing
Rule-Breaking Properties:
  - Virtual mass is CE construct; force is NOT dependent on actual body size or strength
  - Extremely difficult to defend against with conventional physical reinforcement
  - Force differential is simply too large to block through normal CE hardening

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION: YES - CONFIRMED ---
Name: Related to Star Rage concept (precise English name varies by translation)
Mechanics:
  - Used during final battle against Kenjaku
  - Creates a space where virtual mass is applied at maximum potential
  - One of the highest-power domains shown in the series
  - Domain relates to Star Rage applied at full unrestricted scale
Status: Yuki DOES possess a Domain Expansion; used in final confrontation with Kenjaku

8. KOKICHI MUTA (MECHAMARU)
==================================================
Grade: Grade 2 (official); practically Grade 1 level via Mechamaru bodies
Role: Mole within Kyoto, later revealed ally
Notable: Born with severe physical defects due to cursed energy "blessing" trade-off.
         Most of body non-functional; one working arm, one working leg.
         Skin cannot be exposed to moonlight. Operates remotely from hospital.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: PUPPET MANIPULATION ---
Core Mechanic:
  - Kokichi pours cursed energy into puppet bodies and remotely controls them
  - Can operate from any distance (shown across all of Japan)
  - Puppets are his primary combat interface
  - Maintained multiple puppet bodies in different locations simultaneously

The 17 Years of Stored Cursed Energy:
  - Body is severely restricted; cannot physically move/act normally
  - All naturally produced cursed energy has been ACCUMULATING rather than expending
  - 17 years of stored CE = absurdly large reserve
  - Can pour this massive reserve into Mechamaru bodies for power
  - This is why his puppets use techniques that would exhaust a normal sorcerer
  - Effectively has more CE than most active Grade 1 sorcerers

--- MECHAMARU MODES AND TECHNIQUES ---

ULTRA CANNON:
  - Concentrates cursed energy and fires it as high-power energy beam from arm/body
  - Extremely high damage output; long range
  - Beam is a condensed CE projectile
  - Named and used prominently in fight vs. Mahito
  - Damage Type: Condensed cursed energy beam; ranged devastating force

ULTRA SPIN:
  - Mechamaru's arm (or component) rotates at extreme speed
  - Functions as high-velocity spinning strike or drill
  - Can pierce through targets and deflect attacks
  - Rotational force combined with CE makes it effective against physical and CE targets
  - Damage Type: Rotational piercing/blunt force

MODE: ALBATROSS (Ultra Cannon Mouth Variant):
  - Mechamaru transforms upper body/head configuration
  - Opens massive cannon in the mouth/chest area
  - Fires even larger, more powerful energy blast than standard Ultra Cannon
  - "All-in" mode concentrating large energy reserve into single devastating shot
  - Used as trump card against Mahito
  - Damage Type: Massive condensed CE explosion

MODE: ABSOLUTE (Ultimate Mechamaru):
  - Kokichi's ultimate configuration
  - ENTIRE body of Mechamaru converted into single massive weapon system
  - All defensive plating, all stored CE, every component becomes attack platform
  - Puts entire 17-year stored reserve into a single giant body/attack
  - Puppet effectively becomes Special Grade threat in CE output
  - Used in confrontation with Mahito
  - Despite this, Mahito's Idle Transfiguration ultimately defeats it
  - Damage Type: Total system weapons platform; all-in CE output

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
No Domain Expansion shown or referenced for Kokichi Muta.
Combat entirely through Puppet Manipulation and stored CE reservoir.

9. MASTER TENGEN
==================================================
Grade: Special Grade (beyond conventional grading due to age and evolution)
Role: Maintains protective barrier around jujutsu society; foundational infrastructure entity
Notable: Immortal sorcerer maintaining barriers for over 1,000 years. Not a conventional combatant.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: IMMORTALITY ---
Core Mechanic:
  - Does not die from aging or injury
  - Every approximately 500 years, body "EVOLVES" - transcends current form into something new
  - Not resurrection; continuous transformation
  - Consciousness persists through evolution

Star Plasma Vessel Requirement:
  - Evolution normally guided and "reset" by merging with a Star Plasma Vessel
  - Star Plasma Vessel: specific individual born to guide Tengen's evolution back to human-compatible form
  - If merger occurs: Tengen's immortality resets; remains aligned with human consciousness 500 more years
  - If merger DOES NOT occur: Tengen evolves unguided and becomes something inhuman
  - Unguided evolution result: cursed spirit-level entity no longer allied with humanity
  - Riko Amanai was the Star Plasma Vessel; her death (killed by Toji) triggered unguided evolution

Post-Evolution Tengen (Current Manga State):
  - Has evolved beyond human form
  - Still retains barrier techniques and general alignment with jujutsu society
  - Consciousness remains but body is no longer recognizably human
  - Does not actively fight in conventional combat

Damage Type: Not applicable as offensive technique; purely survival/evolution mechanism

--- BARRIER TECHNIQUES ---

Star Corridor / Tombs of the Star Corridor:
  - Network of tunnels beneath jujutsu locations that Tengen maintains
  - Not offensive weapons; structural protective barriers
  - Foundational protective layer around Jujutsu High and jujutsu society infrastructure

Barrier Mastery:
  - Preeminent expert in barrier techniques in the jujutsu world
  - All barrier knowledge in modern jujutsu society traces back to Tengen
  - Can perceive and monitor state of barriers across Japan
  - Can extend and maintain barriers indefinitely (immortality advantage)

Amplification of Sorcerers:
  - In late manga stages, Tengen's CE/barriers used to amplify other sorcerers
  - Sorcerers within his domain of influence receive power amplification
  - Support-type application rather than combat tool

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
No conventional Domain Expansion shown or referenced.
Tengen's existence is more analogous to a living barrier than a fighter.
Star Corridor infrastructure is his "domain" in a metaphorical/structural sense.
Does not use Domain Expansion in combat.

10. MIGUEL ODUOL
==================================================
Grade: Unranked (not part of Japanese jujutsu system); high practical ability
Role: African sorcerer; initially in Pseudo-Geto's faction; later befriends Gojo; trains Yuta
Notable: Uses African sorcery tradition; wields Black Rope cursed tool

--- CURSED TOOL: BLACK ROPE (NKISI) ---
What It Is: African cursed tool; length of rope imbued with CE through African tradition
Differs from Japanese jujutsu tools in construction and properties

Core Property - Cursed Energy Negation/Disruption:
  - Does NOT simply block cursed techniques; ACTIVELY DISRUPTS and negates CE flowing through/past it
  - When Black Rope contacts a cursed technique or CE construct: unravels/cancels that energy
  - Most notably: was able to PARTIALLY disrupt and delay Gojo Satoru's Unlimited Void Domain Expansion
  - Did not fully nullify Gojo's domain but SLOWED and WEAKENED it
  - This is something essentially NO other tool or technique in the series can do against a closed Domain

Against Infinity Specifically:
  - Black Rope can interact with and degrade Infinity's effect at the point of contact
  - Gives it a practical "nullification adjacent" property against Gojo's signature defense

Comparison to Inverted Spear of Heaven:
  - Inverted Spear: nullifies techniques INSTANTLY upon contact
  - Black Rope: disrupts and degrades CE OVER contact time (slower but functions as a weapon)

Finite Supply:
  - Rope is consumed as it is used
  - Miguel burned through enormous lengths fighting Gojo
  - Supply is not unlimited
  - Replacement requires manufacturing through African tradition
  - Damage Type: CE disruption/degradation at point of contact
  - Rule-Breaking Property: Near-unique ability to interfere with top-tier CT including Gojo's Infinity and Unlimited Void

--- TECHNIQUE: PRAYER SONG ---
Mechanic:
  - Miguel's innate cursed technique
  - Draws on African sorcery tradition
  - Involves continuous output of cursed energy sustained through a prayer/song mental state
  - Vocally/mentally activated
Effect:
  - Empowers physical combat
  - Enhances effective range and strength of Black Rope's negation properties
  - Works synergistically with Black Rope
Note: Manga does not give extensive technical detail on precise mechanics beyond synergy with Black Rope
Damage Type: Supplementary CE output; enhances Black Rope's disruption range

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
No Domain Expansion shown or referenced for Miguel Oduol.

11. KASUMI MIWA
==================================================
Grade: Grade 3 Sorcerer
School: Kyoto Jujutsu High, 2nd year
Notable: From non-sorcerer family; became sorcerer to support family financially. Uses katana.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: NEW SHADOW STYLE ---
Classification: Swordsmanship style incorporating cursed energy techniques

NEW SHADOW STYLE: BATTO SWORD DRAW (IAIDO-BASED):
  Mechanic:
    - Single, extremely fast iaido-style draw of sword from scabbard
    - Focuses cursed energy at the moment of the draw
    - Produces single ultra-fast cutting strike
    - Speed is core property: draw happens faster than most opponents can react
    - Committed technique: after the draw, opening phase is over
  Damage Type: Physical cut; lethal if clean hit
  Range: Effective at the draw distance; not a ranged technique

NEW SHADOW STYLE: THREE-FLASH CONSECUTIVE SWORD DRAW:
  Mechanic:
    - Evolution/extension of Batto Sword Draw
    - Three rapid successive draws/cuts performed in sequence
    - Each cut follows the other with minimal gap between them
    - Three strikes come from different angles/trajectories to make defense harder
    - Requires re-sheathing between draws (minimized through training)
  Damage Type: Physical cutting; three separate strikes
  Rule-Breaking Properties: Speed and angle variation make sequential defense difficult

SIMPLE DOMAIN:
  Type: Anti-domain defensive technique (NOT a Domain Expansion)
  Mechanic:
    - Creates small barrier (roughly arm's length radius) around the user
    - Automatically counters ANY technique entering the Simple Domain's range
    - Primary purpose: counter against full Domain Expansions
  Against Full Domain Expansions:
    - When a full Domain is activated, the "sure-hit" effect is negated within Simple Domain range
    - Simple Domain's automatic counter activates FIRST, canceling the sure-hit
  Rule-Breaking Property: Allows sorcerers WITHOUT their own Domain Expansion to SURVIVE inside enemy domains
  Note: Miwa's version is basic; advanced practitioners use more refined versions

Other Techniques:
  - Standard cursed energy reinforcement for speed and strength
  - No other named techniques beyond the above three

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
Simple Domain is NOT a Domain Expansion; it is an anti-domain defensive technique.

12. MAI ZEN'IN
==================================================
Grade: Grade 3 Sorcerer
School: Kyoto Jujutsu High, 2nd year
Notable: Maki's twin sister. Has cursed energy but very little. Opposite restriction to Maki:
         traded physical ability for small CE amount. Not physically strong.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: CONSTRUCTION ---
Core Mechanic:
  - Mai can create a single object from NOTHING using cursed energy
  - She materializes ONE item per day - HARD LIMIT; cannot be increased
  - Creating the object exhausts significant portion of her daily CE reserves

What She Creates:
  - Most commonly: a single bullet (cursed bullet imbued with CE)
  - Capable of damaging cursed spirits
  - Loads into her revolver and fires

Limits (Explicit):
  - ONE object per day: absolute, non-negotiable limit
  - Object must be relatively simple; cannot construct complex machinery
  - Exhausts daily CE after use
  - Created bullet is not dramatically more powerful than standard reinforced projectile
  - Value is convenience and certainty of availability

Final Use of Construction:
  - At her death, Mai uses her LAST act of Construction not to create a bullet
  - Instead: removes final sliver of cursed energy from Maki's body
  - Completes Maki's Heavenly Restriction fully
  - Described as the single most meaningful use of the technique
  - Damage Type: Created object dependent; bullet = physical piercing + CE damage

--- COMBAT LOADOUT ---
Revolver:
  - Standard firearm used in combat
  - Loaded with both standard reinforced cursed bullets AND her single Construction-created bullet
  - Fires from distance; avoids close combat due to limited physical ability

Backup Bullets:
  - Carries pre-loaded cursed bullets from other sources to supplement daily Construction bullet

13. UTAHIME IORI
==================================================
Grade: Semi-Grade 1 Sorcerer (below Grade 1, above Grade 2)
Role: Student supervisor at Kyoto Jujutsu High
Notable: Childhood friend of Mei Mei. Support-type sorcerer. Low offensive combat capability.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE: SOLO FORBIDDEN AREA (SOLITARY FORBIDDEN AREA) ---
Core Mechanic:
  - Amplifies the cursed techniques of allied sorcerers within her range
  - Support-type technique; she is not an offensive combatant

Activation:
  - Utahime SINGS to generate the amplification field
  - Technique is vocally activated and maintained
  - Must continue singing/maintaining to sustain the effect

How Amplification Works:
  - Allied sorcerers within range of her song have CT output amplified
  - Amplification is MULTIPLICATIVE, not additive
  - Significantly multiplies power of techniques being enhanced
  - Not a flat bonus; a true multiplier
  - Exact multiplier not precisely defined but shown to be very large

Stacking / Multiple Uses:
  - Amplification is a sustained effect while she sings/maintains it
  - No clear canon statement on whether it stacks with itself (multiple applications)
  - Does not appear to stack multiplicatively with itself in practice
  - When combined with ANOTHER amplification source, theoretical result is devastating

Practical Application (Final Arc vs. Sukuna):
  - Used to amplify multiple sorcerers simultaneously during coordinated assault on Sukuna
  - Amplification pushes sorcerers above normal power ceiling
  - Makes attacks that would otherwise be insufficient capable of threatening Sukuna

Rule-Breaking Properties:
  - Amplification can push sorcerers TEMPORARILY above their normal power ceiling
  - Indirectly one of the most impactful techniques in large-scale fights
  - Damage Type: Support/amplification; no direct damage output

16. KUSAKABE
==================================================
Grade: Grade 1 Sorcerer
Role: Teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High; veteran combat sorcerer
Notable: Explicitly survival-philosophy fighter. Former colleague of Gojo/Geto generation.
         One of few Grade 1 sorcerers to not possess or show a Domain Expansion.

--- INNATE CURSED TECHNIQUE ---
STATUS: INTENTIONALLY UNDISCLOSED IN MANGA
  - Kusakabe's innate technique is never shown or explicitly explained
  - This is intentional in the narrative
  - As a veteran survival-focused sorcerer, he deliberately conceals his technique's nature
  - Even within jujutsu society, its specifics are unknown
  - Makes him significantly harder to counter in combat (no technique to prepare for)

--- NEW SHADOW STYLE (ADVANCED PRACTITIONER) ---
Level: Master-level practitioner; one of the most proficient NSS users shown

SIMPLE DOMAIN (ADVANCED / MASTER LEVEL):
  Type: Anti-domain defensive technique (NOT a Domain Expansion)
  More Refined Than Basic Practitioners (e.g., Miwa):
    - Larger effective radius
    - More precise automatic countering
    - Used as PRIMARY survival tool in Domain-heavy combat environments
  Against Full Domains:
    - Cancels "sure-hit" effect of Domain Expansions by generating own intercept field
  Against Malevolent Shrine Specifically:
    - During fight against Sukuna, Kusakabe uses Simple Domain to survive Malevolent Shrine range
    - Simple Domain is explicitly one of the FEW tools that can create a survivable bubble
      within an open-type domain (like Malevolent Shrine which has no walls/barrier)
  Teaches Simple Domain:
    - Kusakabe teaches Simple Domain to others including Itadori as a survival technique
    - His expertise in it is considered top-tier among active sorcerers
  Rule-Breaking Property: Cancels sure-hit in enemy domains; survivable bubble in open domains

BATTO SWORD DRAW AND SWORD TECHNIQUES:
  - Master-level execution of iaido strikes
  - Greater power and precision than Miwa's application
  - Standard and extended slash variants available

CURSED ENERGY REINFORCEMENT:
  - At Grade 1 level: excellent reinforcement enhancing physical strikes and defense significantly

--- PHILOSOPHY ---
  - Explicitly survival-first philosophy; repeatedly stated in manga
  - Does not have an innate technique that breaks rules or overpowers enemies
  - Survived to Grade 1 purely through discipline and defensive mastery
  - Uses Simple Domain as core survival mechanism
  - Manga uses him to illustrate that high grades can be reached without raw power gimmicks
  - Archetypal "no domain, no overpowered gimmick, stay alive through skill" veteran sorcerer

--- DOMAIN EXPANSION ---
CONFIRMED: NONE
No Domain Expansion shown or referenced for Kusakabe.
Relies exclusively on Simple Domain as a counter to enemy domains.
